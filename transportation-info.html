Transportation Info

Discover the various ways to travel to and around Gujarat. From air travel to railways and roadways, find the best options for your journey.

By Air

Gujarat is well-connected by air, with major airports in Ahmedabad, Surat, Vadodara, and Rajkot. Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel International Airport in Ahmedabad serves as the primary gateway for international travelers.

  • Direct flights to major Indian cities.
  • International connections to Dubai, London, and more.
  • Convenient airport facilities and services.

By Train

Gujarat boasts an extensive railway network connecting major cities and towns. Travel comfortably in premium trains like Shatabdi Express and Rajdhani Express.

  • Direct trains to Delhi, Mumbai, and Bangalore.
  • Luxury trains like The Palace on Wheels.
  • Convenient online booking and reservation systems.

By Road

Gujarat has an excellent road network, making it easy to explore by car or bus. National highways connect the state to neighboring regions, and luxury buses provide comfort for long-distance travel.

  • State-run and private buses available.
  • Well-maintained highways and toll roads.
  • Scenic drives through picturesque landscapes.
