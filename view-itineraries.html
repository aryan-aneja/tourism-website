Suggested Itineraries for Gujarat

Explore curated travel itineraries for an unforgettable journey through Gujarat, tailored for every type of traveler.

Weekend Getaway

Enjoy a short but memorable trip to destinations like Saputara, Gir National Park, and the Statue of Unity. Perfect for a quick escape!

  • Day 1: Arrival and local sightseeing.
  • Day 2: Explore natural or historical landmarks.

Heritage & Cultural Tour

Delve into Gujarat's rich heritage by visiting historical sites like Somnath Temple, Rani ki Vav, and the old city of Ahmedabad.

  • Day 1: Ahmedabad heritage walk.
  • Day 2: Visit Somnath and Dwarka temples.
  • Day 3: Explore Rani ki Vav and Patan.

Adventure Tour

Discover Gujarat's adventurous side with activities like desert safaris in the Rann of Kutch, hiking in Saputara, and wildlife safaris in Gir.

  • Day 1: Desert safari and cultural programs in Kutch.
  • Day 2: Wildlife safari at Gir National Park.
  • Day 3: Trekking in Saputara hills.
