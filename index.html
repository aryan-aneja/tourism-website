Our Culture

Dive into the vibrant arts, music, dance, and traditions of Gujarat

Folk Dance & Music

Gujarat is synonymous with Garba and Dandiya Raas, energetic folk dances performed especially during Navratri. Traditional music and instruments like dhol, shehnai, and tabla accompany these joyful celebrations.

Art & Craft

The state is famed for handicrafts such as Bandhani textiles, Patola silk, block prints, mirror work, and intricate beadwork from Kutch. These crafts showcase Gujarat's centuries-old artisanship and creativity.

Festivals & Cuisine

From International Kite Festival to Diwali and Holi, festivals are celebrated with color and gusto. Gujarati thali, snacks like dhokla and fafda, and sweets like jalebi are integral to the region's culinary heritage.
